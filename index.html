OTP Fraud

High Risk ▼
How It Works

Fraudster calls posing as bank/Govt official and tricks you into sharing the OTP received on your phone to "verify" your account.

Warning Signs
  • Unsolicited call asking for OTP
  • Urgency — "account will be blocked"
  • Caller claims to be RBI / bank official
Prevention
  • Never share OTP with anyone
  • RBI/banks NEVER ask for OTP on call
  • Hang up immediately
Immediate Action
  • Block your card via bank app
  • Call bank helpline immediately
  • File complaint at cybercrime.gov.in
🚨 Cyber Fraud Helpline: 1930 | cybercrime.gov.in
📷

Fake QR Code Scam

Medium Risk ▼
How It Works

Fraudster pastes a malicious QR sticker over a legitimate shop QR. Payment goes to fraudster, not the merchant.

Warning Signs
  • QR code sticker pasted on top
  • Merchant name doesn't match on UPI confirmation
  • "Scan to receive money" — QR codes only SEND money
Prevention
  • Always verify merchant name on UPI confirmation screen
  • Never scan QR to receive money — QR only sends
  • Check for stickers placed over original QR
🚨 Report to NPCI: 18001201740
🪪

KYC / Account Freeze Scam

High Risk ▼
How It Works

You receive SMS/WhatsApp saying "Complete KYC or your account will be blocked." Link leads to a fake website that steals your banking credentials.

Warning Signs
  • Suspicious link in SMS/WhatsApp
  • Urgency to act within hours
  • Asks for Aadhaar, PAN, OTP on the same page
Prevention
  • Banks do NOT send KYC links via SMS/WhatsApp
  • Visit branch directly for KYC updates
  • Check URL carefully — look for HTTPS
🚨 Report phishing: report.phishing@rbi.org.in
📡

SIM Swap Fraud

High Risk ▼
How It Works

Fraudster convinces your telecom operator to issue a new SIM with your number. They then receive all your OTPs and drain your bank account.

Warning Signs
  • Your mobile signal suddenly disappears
  • Calls/SMS stop working
  • Unexpected bank alerts about transactions
Prevention
  • Never share your Aadhaar/address with unknown callers
  • Enable SIM lock with your telecom provider
  • Set transaction alerts via email too
Immediate Action
  • Call telecom operator immediately to block SIM
  • Call bank to freeze account
  • File FIR at police station
🚨 Helpline: 1930 | cybercrime.gov.in
💹

Investment / Ponzi Scam

High Risk ▼
How It Works

Telegram/WhatsApp groups promise "guaranteed 30–50% returns" on investments. Initial small returns build trust, then large sums are taken and the fraudster disappears.

Warning Signs
  • Guaranteed high returns (no investment guarantees returns)
  • Pressure to recruit others
  • Not registered with SEBI
  • Requests payment in crypto or wallets
Prevention
  • Verify SEBI registration at sebi.gov.in
  • Never invest based on Telegram tips
  • If it sounds too good — it's a scam
🚨 Report to SEBI SCORES: scores.gov.in | 1800227575
🤖

AI Voice / Deepfake Scam

Emerging Threat ▼
How It Works

AI clones the voice of your family member or boss using social media audio. Calls you urgently requesting a money transfer for an "emergency."

Warning Signs
  • Unusual urgency in voice call
  • Slight robotic tone or unnatural pauses
  • Request for immediate wire transfer
Prevention
  • Hang up and call back on the known number
  • Establish a secret family code word
  • Never transfer money without in-person/video verification
🚨 Report: cybercrime.gov.in | Helpline: 1930
🖥️

Screen Sharing App Scam

High Risk ▼
How It Works

Fraudster posing as "bank customer care" asks you to install AnyDesk or TeamViewer. Once installed, they view your banking app and steal credentials/funds.

Warning Signs
  • Customer care asking to install any app
  • Requests for screen share access
  • Unknown person guides you step by step
Prevention
  • Banks NEVER ask you to install screen-sharing apps
  • Hang up immediately
  • Uninstall app & change all passwords at once
🚨 Helpline: 1930 | Report: cybercrime.gov.in
